What Is MiaCasa?

MiaCasa is a boutique homestay in Hanoi — not a large hotel chain, not a faceless rental. We are local hosts who live in the city and personally manage two distinct stays in different parts of Hanoi.

We're also listed on Airbnb, Booking.com, Agoda, Trip.com, and DayLaDau, where guests have rated us 9.3 and awarded us Superhost status. But when you book directly through our website, you get better rates, more flexibility, and direct communication with the people who actually host you.

MiaCasa sits between a hotel and an Airbnb stay. You get the privacy and independence of a rental, plus the attention and local knowledge of hosts who genuinely care about your experience.

More Personal Than a Hotel

Hotels often provide consistency, while Airbnb stays can vary from host to host. MiaCasa combines the comfort of a private stay with the support of local hosts.

We offer:

  • Local hosts who understand Hanoi and genuinely care about your stay
  • Direct communication before, during, and after your stay
  • Thoughtfully designed spaces — not staged for photos, but lived-in and comfortable
  • Better value through direct booking with no platform commissions

Over the years, we have welcomed solo travelers, couples, families, remote workers, and long-term visitors from dozens of countries. Many guests arrive as visitors and leave feeling connected to the city in a completely different way.
