The Story Behind MiaCasa — A Boutique Homestay in Hanoi

MiaCasa did not begin as a business plan. It began as a simple idea of creating a place that feels calm, warm, and genuinely lived in.

✨ Created slowly in Hanoi for travelers who want a stay that feels personal, calm, and real.

← MiaCasa Homestays

MiaCasa is a boutique homestay in Hanoi offering curated stays in both a quiet local area near Văn Miếu (Temple of Literature), Hanoi Train Street, and Ba Đình, plus a central Old Quarter apartment close to Hoàn Kiếm Lake.

Meet the Hosts

MiaCasa is built and hosted by Linh and Ngọc, two long-time friends who share a love for thoughtful hospitality and intentional spaces.

Linh

Linh is a professional interior designer who focuses on creating warm, comfortable environments with natural textures, soft lighting, and quiet details. Her philosophy is simple: design spaces where people genuinely want to slow down and stay.

Ngọc

Ngọc works in hospitality and focuses on the guest experience behind the scenes — communication, comfort, flexibility, and the small details that make a stay feel smooth and personal.

Together, they built MiaCasa gradually, learning and refining along the way.

Linh shapes the spaces. Ngọc shapes the experience.

MiaCasa was never designed to feel like a hotel. It was designed to feel like a home people remember.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is MiaCasa a hotel or homestay?
MiaCasa is a boutique homestay, not a hotel. Guests enjoy a private and independent stay while still experiencing a warm, local atmosphere. The hosts do not live inside the property, but our team is always available online or nearby if you need anything during your stay.
Which MiaCasa property is better for couples?
MiaCasa Hanoi is ideal for couples and solo travelers. It's a quieter neighborhood stay near Văn Miếu and Train Street with private studio-style rooms and kitchenettes.
Is direct booking cheaper than Airbnb or Booking.com?
Yes. Direct booking through our website offers better rates because there are no platform commissions. You get the same trusted MiaCasa experience at a lower price.
How far is MiaCasa from Hanoi Old Quarter?
MiaCasa Hanoi is located near Văn Miếu, about 10-15 minutes from the Old Quarter. MiaCasa Old Quarter is located right in the heart of the Old Quarter itself.
Do you offer airport pickup?
Yes, we can arrange airport pickup for guests. Please contact us directly after booking and we'll help arrange transportation from Noi Bai International Airport.
